
<p>In September 2016 Daniel Harding takes up his appointment as Music Director of the Orchestre de Paris in their magnificent new concert hall, the Philharmonie.&nbsp;&nbsp; He retains his posts as Music Director of the Swedish Radio Symphony Orchestra, Principal Guest Condcutor of the LondonSymphony Orchestra and Music Partner of the New Japan Philharmonic. &nbsp; &nbsp;He was recently honoured with the lifetime title of Conductor Laureate of the Mahler Chamber Orchestra.&nbsp;&nbsp; He has made many acclaimed recordings, most recently Mahler&rsquo;s Symphony No. 10 with the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra and Orff&rsquo;s Carmina Burana with the Bavarian Radio Symphony Orchestra.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The task:&nbsp;&nbsp; PR support both nationally and internationally.</p>
